Rob Zombie!

Bio

Rob Zombie is an American heavy metal musician who rose to fame as the singer and founder of White Zombie. After releasing four successful albums with White Zombie, Rob went solo in 1998. Shortly after his first solo album was released, White Zombie disbanded. Rob has a rotating schedule of backing musicians, including some prominent members of other rock bands.

His debut album , Hellbilly Deluxe, sold over 3 million copies worldwide. In addition to music, Rob Zombie is also a successful filmmaker and producer. His music and films all tend to have a horror or sci-fi theme, and he could be referred to as a shock rocker.
